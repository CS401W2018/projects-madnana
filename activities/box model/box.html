Block Tags

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags.
Block tags take up the full width of their container and always start on a new line. They control larger sections of the layout.Inline tags only take up as much space as their content and stay on the same line as other inline elements.
What is the margin, border, and padding?
Margin: The space outside an element's border. It separates the element from other elements.
Border: The line that goes around the padding and content of an element.
Padding: The space between the element's content and its border.
What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?
px (pixels): Fixed size, used for precise control.
em and rem: Relative to the font size (of the parent or root element).
% (percent): Relative to the parent element's size.
vh and vw: Viewport height and width, based on the size of the browser window.
