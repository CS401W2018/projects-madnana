Fundamentals of Cooking

Measure Accurately:
Always use measuring cups for dry ingredients and measuring jugs for liquids. Precision is key to baking success.
Room Temperature Ingredients:
Many recipes work best with ingredients like butter and eggs at room temperature to ensure smooth mixing.
Preheat Your Oven:
Always preheat your oven to the specified temperature before baking to ensure even cooking.

Fundamentals of Baking

Folding Technique:
Learn to gently fold ingredients to maintain air in your batter for light and fluffy results.
Kneading Dough:
Master the art of kneading to develop gluten and achieve perfectly elastic dough for bread.
Piping Tips:
Use a piping bag with different nozzles to decorate cakes and cupcakes like a pro.
