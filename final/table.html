Pudim de Pão, also known as Bread Pudding, is a classic dessert made using day-old pão francês (Brazilian French bread). If you're in a region where pão francês isn't available, don't worry! Pedro Brito from Brazilian Kitchen has created a special video to show you how to make this essential bread at home. Follow his tips to bring authentic flavors to your cooking!

Craving a savory snack? Coxinha, Brazil's famous chicken-filled pastry, is a crowd-pleaser that's crispy on the outside and creamy on the inside. In this video from Brazilian Kitchen by Pedro Brito, you'll learn the secrets to making this delicious treat at home. Follow along and discover why Coxinha is a favorite street food across Brazil!
