Banana Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 dozen and a half bananas
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Cinnamon (to taste)

Dough:

  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 cup of wheat flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons margarine

instructions:

  1. If the egg is very large, increase the amount of flour.
  2. The texture of the dough is easy to determine, and once you get it right, it is delicious!

Shrimp Vatapá

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg shrimp
  • 1 bottle coconut milk
  • 1 box rice cream (Colombo brand)
  • Olive oil and palm oil
  • Chopped tomatoes, green seasoning, onion, and garlic
  • Malagueta pepper (to taste)

instructions:

  1. Sauté the shrimp and coconut milk with all the ingredients for the sauce.
  2. Dissolve 90% of the rice cream in water to create a paste.
  3. Mix the paste into the shrimp sauce and cook, stirring continuously, until it starts bubbling.
  4. Once cooked, add 2 tablespoons of palm oil and 1 pinch of malagueta pepper.
  5. When making the shrimp sauce, use a bit of olive oil for a special flavor.

Bolo de Côco Gelado

Ingredients Cake Base:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 6 egg whites (beaten until stiff)

Ingredients for the Syrup:

  • 1 bottle of coconut milk
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can of cow's milk

instructions for the Syrup:

  1. Blend the syrup ingredients in a blender and pour over the cake while it is still warm.

Ingredients Topping:

  • 4 egg whites (beaten until stiff)
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 can of heavy cream

instructions for the Topping:

  1. Spread the topping on the cold cake. If desired, sprinkle grated coconut on top.

Bolinho de Chuva

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 heaping tablespoon of baking powder
  • 6 tablespoons of sugar

instructions:

  1. Beat the egg whites until stiff and set aside. Beat the sugar and egg yolks together.
  2. Add the egg whites, and gradually mix in the flour.
  3. If the batter becomes too thick, add a little milk. Mix the baking powder with the flour.
  4. Let the batter rest for about 15 minutes.

Xodó da Bahia

Ingredients:

  • 1 large box of vanilla flan
  • 1 liter of milk
  • 1 box of shredded coconut (200g)
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 1 can of condensed milk

instructions:

  1. Boil the milk and coconut until well blended.
  2. Dissolve the flan in a small amount of milk. Mix the condensed milk with the 4 already-beaten eggs.
  3. Combine everything with the boiled milk and, finally, add the flan mixture.
  4. Pour into a caramelized mold and chill in the refrigerator.
