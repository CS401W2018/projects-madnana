Stephen King

Stephen King stands out as a truly unique storyteller because of his unmatched ability to blend the ordinary with the terrifying. His characters are relatable, and he masterfully builds suspense, making readers feel invested in their struggles. King has a talent is making the supernatural seem real bringing the Uncanny valley to life. While also exploring the darkness of the human mind.

Three example of his work are:

  • The Shinning

    • Explores the psychological breakdown of a man haunted by supernatural forces in an isolated hotel with his family.

  • Strong

    • Tells the story of a cursed man fighting against time to try to break a Romani curse before it is too late.

  • It

    • The story follows a group of children battling an ancient, shape-shifting evil that feeds on fear in their small town.
